FAQ's

How do I place an order on your website?

To place an order, simply browse our menu, select the items you'd like, and proceed to the checkout. Follow the prompts to enter your delivery details and payment information.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and other online payment options. The available methods are displayed during the checkout process.

Is it possible to schedule an order for later in the day?

Yes, many restaurants offer the option to schedule your order for a later time. During checkout, you can select your preferred delivery time.

Can I cancel my order after it's been placed?

The ability to cancel an order depends on the restaurant's policies and the stage of preparation. You can attempt to cancel through the website or by contacting customer support as soon as possible.

Do you charge for delivery?

The delivery fee varies based on your location and the restaurant. The delivery fee will be displayed during the checkout process before you confirm your order.
